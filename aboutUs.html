ARCANA est.2005

ARCANA Landscaping is a trusted and reputable landscaping company that has been serving clients since its establishment in 2005. With a passion for creating stunning outdoor spaces, we take pride in our expertise, professionalism, and commitment to exceeding our clients' expectations.

Jonathan Mitchell - Founder and Lead Designer

Jonathan Mitchell, the visionary behind ARCANA Landscaping, has been a driving force in the industry for over 20 years. His deep understanding of design principles and horticulture allows him to create captivating landscapes that blend seamlessly with nature. With Jonathan's attention to detail and commitment to quality, he ensures that each project reflects the unique vision and personality of our clients.

The Journey

Since its humble beginnings in 2005, ARCANA Landscaping has experienced remarkable growth and success in the landscaping industry. What started as a small venture has evolved into a leading landscaping company renowned for its exceptional services and commitment to customer satisfaction.

At the heart of ARCANA Landscaping is its visionary founder and owner, Johnathan Mitchell. With a deep-rooted passion for landscaping and a keen eye for design, Johnathan has been the driving force behind the company's growth and success since its inception in 2005.

Under Johnathan's guidance, ARCANA Landscaping has grown from a one-person operation to a thriving team of skilled professionals. His hands-on approach and collaborative leadership style have fostered a culture of innovation, teamwork, and client satisfaction within the company.

Through years of dedication and hard work, ARCANA has steadily expanded its operations, serving a wide range of clients throughout the Gold Coast, Brisbane, and the Tweed Coast. Our reputation for delivering high-quality results and personalized solutions has allowed us to forge strong relationships with homeowners, businesses, and property developers in the region.

Looking ahead, we are dedicated to further expanding our reach and enhancing our service offerings. We strive to stay at the forefront of industry advancements, embracing sustainable practices and innovative technologies to create landscapes that are not only visually appealing but also environmentally friendly.

As we continue on our journey of growth, ARCANA Landscaping remains committed to providing top-quality landscaping services, personalized attention to detail, and a passion for creating outdoor spaces that inspire and delight our clients.
